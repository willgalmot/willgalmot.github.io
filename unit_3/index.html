MelID

Whistle the tune, and you'll get the name soon!

Ever have catchy tunes stuck in your head...but get all frustrated 'cause you can't name the title of the song? Well, with MelID installed on your device, you can put in as little as 5 seconds of a melody, and that'll be a problem no more! Play it out loud, whistle it, hum it--and it'll give you a whole list of all songs that it could be!


Once you've got it on your device, you pick one of these five plans:

  • Basic Plan: Totally Free of Charge, 3 ads for every third tune.
  • Silver Plan: $5 per month, 3 ads for every tenth tune.
  • Gold Plan: $10 per month, 3 ads for every 30th tune.
  • Platinum Plan: $20 per month, 1 ad for every 30th tune, free mp3 download for the songs that match your tunes.
  • Premium Plan: $40 per month, no ads, free mp3 download for the entire album of the songs that match your tunes...plus the instrumental/acapella versions!
